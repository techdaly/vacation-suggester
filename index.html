Earth

When you're looking for a low-key getaway, there's no place like home. On Earth you will find gorgeous terrain of all types, and many of it can be reached on a small budget.


Venus

Nothing in the known universe can compare to the romance and elegance of Venus. You can enjoy the sights off the observation deck after only a brief trek from Earth. Be sure to try the mimosas!


Europa

Travel to the mysterious Europa and prepare to be astounded by its mysterious, watery depths. Deep sea scuba diving and close encounters with giant marine fauna are just a few of the activities you can expect, but on Europa, the excitement is in what you can't expect.


Kepler 186f

If you're craving a challenge to push you to the edge of your limits, try hiking the upper reaches of Kepler 186f. Make sure to keep your stamina up on the long flight out! These red forests aren't for the weak!


Kepler 16b

Kepler 16b is for the traveler who is ready to go as far as they can go. Not much is known about the adventures that await at the other end of the galaxy, but we asure you that its a perfectly safe choice. Make sure to sign our waiver before you depart.


PSO J318.5-22

Get ready to party where the night never ends on PSO J318.5-22. This planet has no sun, and the night lasts all day. Between the casinos and the high class bars, you'll always have a bright distraction.
